Ukrainian borsch

On 5-6 liters of water we need:

cabbage - half a head
onions - 1-2 heads
beet - 1 piece
carrots 2-3 pieces
potatoes 4-5 pieces
garlic 4-5 cloves
and 2 pounds of meat

Step 1

Wash the meat and cut it into small pieces.

Step 2

Cut the beet into thin strips

Step 3

Put the meat into a saucepan with boiling water and bring to a boil.
Then I put out the dirty water, rinse the meat, fill it with fresh water and bring it to a boil. I do this so that the broth is transparent.

Step 4

Add the beet into a saucepan with boiling water and bring to a boil.

Step 5

Cut the onions and carrots into small cubes

Step 6

Pour oil into a frying pan and fry the onion until golden brown

Step 7

Then add the carrots to the pan, mix and fry a little more, then add 2 tablespoons of flour, mix, fry for about 1 minute and send all this to the saucepan.

Step 8

Add bay leaf, pepper, garlic, salt (2 tablespoons) to the saucepan.

Step 9

Shred the cabbage, cut the potatoes into small cubes and add to the saucepan.

Step 10

Cut the tomatoes and add them to the saucepan.

Step 11

Bring to a boil, reduce heat and cook for 25-30 minutes. It's all. The borsch is ready. We add sour cream to the plate to taste.
